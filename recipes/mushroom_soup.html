Mushroom Soup

Description

large, rectangular strips of pasta.

a baked dish consisting of layers of this pasta, cheese, tomato sauce, and usu. ground meat.

Ingredients

    Puck Bechamel Sauce with Cheese 250mg Leek, white part only, finely sliced 1 piece Garlic clove, crushed 2 pieces Button mushroom, finely sliced or chopped 500g Chicken stock 2 cups Salt and pepper to taste Nutmeg Thyme sprigs 2 pieces

Steps

    Melt butter in a large saucepan. Add the mushrooms and sauté until they get a nice golden color (about 5 minutes).

    Add the leeks and garlic, then cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally until softened.

    Add sprigs of thyme, then add the chicken stock and Puck Bechamel Sauce with Cheese and bring to a boil. Simmer for 2 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.

    Lightly grease the mixing bowl with some oil. Put the kneaded dough back in, cover the bowl with a clean tea towel and leave the dough to rise until doubled in size. Preheat your oven to 240°C (fan 220°C, gas mark 9).

    Remove the thyme sprigs, then blend the soup until its creamy and smooth.

    Ladle into warm bowls and garnish with parsley leaves.
